Customer segments

Design the lifecycle by segment, because needs and buying behavior differ significantly. One framework, three distinct execution modes.

Residential

Homeowners, renters & property managers

Fast response, trust and safety, visible results, affordable ongoing prevention.

  • Cockroaches

Stage 04

Service

Deliver reliable outcomes, build long-term trust, and prevent churn.

Customer mindset

  • Did you solve the problem?
  • Can I trust this company long term?
  • Do I still need this?

Visit structure

  • 1. Inspection
  • 2. Identification of pest and root causes
  • 3. Treatment / intervention
  • 4. Prevention recommendations
  • 5. Documented report
  • 6. Scheduling of next action

Reports & IPM

  • Digital service reports with photos and trend data
  • SMART monitoring data integrated
  • Inspection and monitoring first, targeted treatment only when needed
  • Sanitation, proofing/exclusion recommendations
  • Biodiversity Impact Matrix guiding biocide decisions
  • Customer education on prevention

Proactive engagement

  • Upcoming visit reminders
  • Seasonal risk increase alerts
  • Service due date notifications
  • Renewal window communications
  • Prevention Score as ongoing value proof
  • Compliance reminders for regulated industries

Consistency & exceptions

  • Global SOPs with local adaptation
  • Brand standards, PPE, and safety protocols
  • Service quality scoring per branch
  • Fast re-service for re-infestation risk
  • Root cause review and supervisor escalation
  • Technical specialist involvement when needed

Churn scoring

  • Missed appointments and declining engagement
  • Complaint and re-service frequency
  • Price sensitivity signals
  • Contract age and multi-service adoption
  • NPS/CSAT trends

Retention interventions

  • Branch Manager service review call
  • Adjusted service frequency
  • Loyalty pricing
  • Free inspection add-on or SMART upgrade

Stage 05

Loyalty

Grow customer value, build advocacy, and recover at-risk relationships.

Customer mindset

  • What else can you help with?
  • I’d recommend these guys.
  • Give me a reason to come back.

Expansion by segment

  • Residential: termite, mosquito, rodent exclusion, wildlife, SMART upgrade
  • Commercial: audit support, hygiene monitoring, staff training, digital upgrades
  • Enterprise: global contract expansion, new-country rollout, analytics packages

Trigger-based upsell

  • Technician-led lead capture during visits
  • Upsells triggered by pest sightings, season, property type
  • Inside sales follow-up and account review meetings
  • QBRs for enterprise accounts
  • Targeted email/SMS campaigns

Reviews & referrals

  • Review requests at first resolution and renewal anniversaries
  • Reviews tied to branch location for local SEO
  • Residential refer-a-friend credit
  • B2B referral incentives
  • Partner referral agreements with property managers

Community trust

  • Vertical-specific case studies and testimonials
  • Infestation reduction and audit success narratives
  • Local educational events and school outreach
  • Sustainability and biodiversity protection messaging

Complaint recovery

  • Acknowledge within defined SLA
  • Investigate root cause, act, and confirm resolution
  • Tiered escalation: frontline → Branch Manager → technical expert → regional director
  • Structured post-mortems for lost enterprise accounts

Win-back

  • Price: lower-tier plan or SMART value justification
  • Inactivity: seasonal reactivation reminder
  • Poor experience: Branch Manager-led personal outreach
  • Moved/closed site: new property onboarding
  • Learnings fed back into lifecycle improvements

Conversion & Onboarding

  • Standardize service reports

Phase 3

Retention & Expansion

Months 7–9
  • Deploy renewal engine
  • Implement churn scoring

Global Optimization

  • Benchmark countries/branches

09

Risks and mitigations

Key risks that could undermine the lifecycle model and the mitigations built into the plan.

Risk

One-time customers do not convert to recurring

Mitigation
  • Default to plan bundles with SMART monitoring included
  • Educate on prevention economics
  • Offer warranties tied to recurring plans
Risk

Inconsistent service across countries and branches

Mitigation
  • Global SOPs with local adaptation
  • Technician certification programs
  • QA audits and shared dashboards
  • Branch competition benchmarking
Risk

High churn after pest issue appears solved

Mitigation
  • Seasonal education campaigns
  • Prevention Score as ongoing value proof
  • Flexible plan tiers
  • Proactive reminders
Risk

Weak enterprise reporting

Mitigation
  • Central data model with mandatory site-level logging
  • Standard global dashboard
  • Regular QBR cadence with Platform accountability
Risk

Regulatory non-compliance across markets

Mitigation
  • Local compliance ownership at Platform level
  • Approved treatment libraries (Biodiversity Impact Matrix)
  • Country-level legal review of messaging and methods
